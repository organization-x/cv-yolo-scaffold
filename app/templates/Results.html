Our model isn't perfect.
Machine learning is an incredibly complicated field to master, and given we only had 3 weeks to learn AI from scratch, errors are bound to happen. This meant that our data set was somewhat limited and may have bias that influenced our model's predicted potholes.

To mitigate these problems as much as possible, we generated confusion matrices of our model to evaluate how well it was doing and used our validation sets to fine tune our model to perform as best as it possibility can in our limited time frame.
